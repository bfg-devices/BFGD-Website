ABOUT

CUSTOM ARTWORK. HAND-BUILT. PEDALS, AMPS AND MORE. MADE IN THE U.S.A.

B.F.G. Devices is a brand abbreviation of “Brandon Frenzel Guitar Devices”. I started building my own pedals before the COVID-19 pandemic and found it to be a most liberating hobby.

I specialize in using system integration techniques to produce boutique, hand-built guitar/bass pedals as well as some more ambitious projects like SFF (small form factor) amplifiers like the BFG200G.

I’m a veteran of the musical instrument industry, having designed and worked on dozens of products over the last decade. I want to share my art and design skills with other creative musicians and build them something unique.

I've recently begun creating original circuit designs and combining them with new artwork. I'd love to design a pedal for you!
