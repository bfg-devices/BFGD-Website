Icarus Takes Flight

January 31, 2023
"You make guitar pedals right?"

"I do!", I quickly replied.

After a conversation he inquired if I could build him a pedal to use with a synthesizer, to match his Prophet that he intended to order. He had never owned a guitar pedal before for the purposes of using with a synth and was looking for something unique. I was happy to oblige him and we settled terms for me to build it.

Fog Gate

January 31, 2023
"Use the stonesword key"

One essential part of my signal chain is the noise gate. Up until now, I only had a cheap Neewer (Amazon) noise gate which for the most part; did the job. I wasn't yet able to afford one of the now-discontinued Fortin Zuul noise gates which are unique -they are placed in the effects loop, but have the option to be "keyed' by the guitar input.

Phase Runner

January 31, 2023
"Or is it merely thy habit, to talk to dolls?"

This was probably my quickest build yet, which is based an MXR Phase 90. It aims to clone both the block and script models and has a toggle for the number of stages, which provides some interesting variations of phaser effects.

Moon Chorus

January 31, 2023
"Or is it merely thy habit, to talk to dolls?"

Ever since beginning my journey in DIY Pedals and collecting guitar pedals, I was enamored by the amount of pedalboard pictures I'd seen that had a Walrus Audio Julia on them. There was something very alluring about the artwork that made it very desirable. It brought a dark aesthetic to the boards and made me think of pedalboards as not only tools of musical expression, but of artistic expression as well.
