Hydra Delay

January 31, 2023
Multi-Headed Delay Machine

Overview

After building my first kit pedal - The AION FX Refractor, I got the itch to build another pedal. Something harder, and to actually try sourcing all of the parts myself, so I could inject some more creativity into it. I had my eye on the PedalPCB "Hyrda" for some time. I needed a delay on my board that wasn't part of a multi-effect. Because apparently I like doing things the hard way.
